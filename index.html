About Protena Group

We're a global company operating in Latin America for over 40 years. Our adult bovine blood processing plants are located in Nicaragua, Bolivia, and Colombia. We operate in Mexico, Central and South America, Taiwan, and the Caribbean. Our aim is to transform organic by-products from animal facilities into high-nutrition proteins for food and fertilizer production, providing an environmental solution.

We strive to create innovative blood products that improve the environment, nutrition, and health, guided by our values of performance, responsibility, innovation, and transparency. Together, we nurture the future.
